Tutorial
  1. Sign in to Bing with your Microsoft Rewards account and return to this page.
  2. Start a new auto search by clicking the "Start Auto Search" button.
  3. The progress counter can be viewed on the "Stop Auto Search" button.
  4. To perform mobile searches, use your smartphone or emulate a mobile browser through the developer tools (F12) on the PC.

Multi-tab Mode

Be aware! To use the multi-tab mode, it's necessary to allow the opening of multiple tabs/pop-ups in your browser.

Due to system restrictions, for some devices/browsers it may be necessary to open multiple tabs to search and receive rewards points (enable if this is your case).

When enabling multi-tab mode, it's recommended to use a longer interval (30sec+) between searches, as opening new tabs depends on and may affect your device's performance quality.
